About Lord of the Drinks, Connaught Place (CP), Central Delhi

Going old school with pubs has become a major trend in gastronomy world and by bringing back the vintage times we have hit upon a jackpot. But here we are talking about this fantastic chain of pubs that has burst upon Delhi’s night scene. When we say old school, forget about the British waterholes or Raj era inspired old-fashioned bars. We are talking about going back centuries in western timeline when fields were strewn with wars and sword carrying warriors.
